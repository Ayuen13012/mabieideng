Pan-Akuot Clearing and Forwarding Company, based in Nimule, South Sudan, is a leading logistics company specializing in import and export services. Location.

The company is situated within Nimule Juba South Sudan, one of the busiest company in Africa. This proximity allows for quick and efficient transfer of goods from ships to the company’s facilities. The company has access to several large warehousing facilities nearby, which are equipped with modern storage solutions to handle a variety of goods, including perishables and hazardous materials.
